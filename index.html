Zodiac

Zodiac is a 2007 American mystery thriller film directed by David Fincher. It tells the story of the manhunt for the Zodiac Killer,
a serial murderer who terrorized the San Francisco Bay Area during the late 1960s and early 1970s, taunting police with letters, bloodstained clothing, and ciphers mailed to newspapers.
The case remains one of the United States' most infamous unsolved crimes.
